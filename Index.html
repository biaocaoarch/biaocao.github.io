Biao Cao is an architectural designer with over 5 years of professional experience across China and the US. He holds a Master of Architecture from the Southern California Institute of Architecture (SCI-Arc), where he received a Graduate Thesis Merit Award.

During his tenure at firms like Arquitectonica, he has contributed to landmark projects including the 1420 South Miami Ave、Miami Airport Wetin Hotel and Starfield Jinju Shopping Mall. He focuses on leveraging computational tools to bridge the gap between architectural logic and artistic expression.
